FAQ/About

What is this?

How much is your Clash of Clans account worth? Find out now by entering your tag above.

How does it work?

We count the value of your account based on the number of resources, buildings, and troops you have etc.

Why do we need your tag?

We need your tag to access your account information and calculate the value of your account.

Is it safe?

Yes, it is safe. We do not store your tag or any other information. We only use it to calculate the value of your account and then show it to you. We don't save any data.

How accurate is it?

It is very accurate. We use the official Clash of Clans API to calculate the value of your account. We compare your account with other accounts and calculate the value based on that.
